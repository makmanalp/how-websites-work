The Semantics of Types

Identifiers

(Not numbers!)

  • 617-555-1234
  • 721-07-1426
  • 01605

Categoricals

(Not strings!)

  • red, green, blue, orange
  • A, B, AB, 0
  • noun, verb, adjective, adverb

Ordinals

(Also not strings!)

  • low, medium, high
  • ★, ★★, ★★★, ★★★★
  • high school, undergrad, graduate
